Welcome to Rinda Rwanda

Rinda Rwanda is the voice of the voiceless, the eyes of the unseen opportunities, and the steps for children with intellectual disabilities. We believe protection is not about weapons, but love, sacrifice, and compassion in action for those forgotten or ignored.

About Us

The name "RINDA RWANDA" symbolizes protection with dignity and purpose. It reflects our commitment to shielding and uplifting children who often go unseen in society, advocating for their rights and inclusion with love and action.

Our Mission

To protect, empower, and inspire children with intellectual disabilities by creating safe, inclusive, and nurturing environments through education, care, and advocacy in Rwanda.
